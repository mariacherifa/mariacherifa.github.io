I am a third-year Ph.D. Student at ENSAE-CREST and Criteo AI Lab, under the supervision of [Vianney Perchet](https://www.ensae.fr/faculty/670-vianney-perchet) and [Clément Calauzènes](https://scholar.google.ca/citations?user=lFsKnyUAAAAJ&hl=en). My research focuses on online learning on algorithms, with applications in digital advertising. Before my PhD, I graduated from the [master MVA (ENS Cachan)](https://www.master-mva.com) in 2021, with a major in machine learning.
